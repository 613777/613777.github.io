Customer Churn Analysis Report

1. Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn using a telecom dataset. The goal is to understand factors that influence whether a customer will leave the service.

2. Data Description

The dataset includes customer information such as tenure (length of stay), monthly charges, total charges, and churn status (Yes/No).

3. Data Analysis

We used SQL queries and data analysis techniques to explore the dataset. Key variables such as tenure and monthly charges were analyzed to identify patterns.

4. Key Findings

  • Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.
  • Customers with shorter tenure have higher churn rates.
  • Long-term customers tend to stay with the service.

5. Conclusion

This analysis shows that pricing and customer retention strategies are important factors in reducing churn. Businesses can use these insights to improve customer satisfaction.
